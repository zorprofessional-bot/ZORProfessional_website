# ZOR Professional Project Brief

## Brand

ZOR Professional is a Croatian toilet paper brand by ZOR d.o.o. The public website is a no-scroll product deck: a premium interactive presentation for a practical production brand, not a long corporate website, catalogue, or webshop.

## Audience

Primary users are households, small companies, apartment owners, schools, institutions, and smaller communities that need reliable toilet paper supply, a fair price, and simple ordering. A secondary audience is production workers considering work at Robni terminali Jankomir.

## Core Message

Croatian production, reliable availability, fair price, and simple ordering from Robni terminali Jankomir.

## Website Model

The public `/hr` and `/en` routes use a chapter-and-slide deck model.

- Main navigation changes the chapter: Home, Products, Production, Calculator, Blog, Careers, Contact.
- Next/previous controls, keyboard arrows, mouse wheel throttling, and mobile swipe change slides inside the current chapter.
- The URL stores slide state with `?slide=...`.
- Public deck pages fit inside `100svh`; normal document scrolling is disabled.
- Blog article pages may use an internal reader panel for long text.

## Current Chapters

- Home: hero, audience, why ZOR, and quick path.
- Products: overview, ZOR 24 Everyday, ZOR 24 Softer, ZOR 36 Supply, and business quantities.
- Production: Croatian production, jumbo roll, rewinding, cutting, packing, and warehouse availability.
- Calculator: intro, input form, result, and WhatsApp inquiry.
- Blog: featured posts, practical guides, and production/buying advice.
- Careers: production-line learning, operator role, training, open roles, and application.
- Contact: WhatsApp, contact form, and Robni terminali Jankomir location.

## Exclusions

Supabase is now allowed as the optional content and lead backend for chapters, slides, products, blog posts, careers, settings, leads, applications, and storage. Do not add Shopify, checkout, cart flows, a full admin UI, pricing automation, or complex business logic at this stage. Product prices are placeholder package prices until confirmed.

## Tone

Clear, direct, premium but approachable. Copy should be short and useful, with natural SEO phrasing around toilet paper, wc paper, Croatian production, price, apartments, 24-roll and 36-roll packs, and business supply.
